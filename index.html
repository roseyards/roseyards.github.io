About Us

Our congregation is part of the Presbyterian Church in Ireland. You can find us on the Kirk Road, about 3 miles from Ballymoney and 1 mile from Stranocum.

There’s no typical Roseyards person. We’re ‘all sorts’ – different ages, personalities, backgrounds, tastes. We like to think we’re a friendly congregation. Of course, being a church, we have some core beliefs that are vitally important.

If you’d like to know more about these, please go to ‘what we believe.’ Hope to see you soon!
